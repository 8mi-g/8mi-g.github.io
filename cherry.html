2019 Event Poster

Cherry Blossom Festival

Hypothetical solution to event poster project. This is a Gif poster designed for the 2019 Cherry Blossom Festival in Japan.

This poster was designed to be displayed on motion supportive billboards or printed posters around the city of Japan to promote the Cherry Blossom Festival in Honshu. The motions of the poster aim to represent the feeling of blossom trees growing, blooming and falling.

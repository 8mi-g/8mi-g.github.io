2020 Misinformation

3ore Project

As we become exposed to all the information on the internet, we are also exposed to misinformation. However, it is always import for us to access and know about truth. The project of 3ore encourages audience to know more about business scandals and aims to reveal the true story of three famous business scandals.

3ore Project documents the cases of Bre-X, Theranos and Enron and includes 4 posters and an interactive website. The posters would be displayed on busy business areas to attract attention and visitors to the website.
